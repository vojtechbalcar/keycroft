# TypingWithAllTen Idea Bank

Date: 2026-03-31
Format: numbered idea bank for quick reference
Primary direction: cozy mastery world
Working name: Keycroft

## Snapshot

This document is intentionally broad. The goal is not to ship every idea. The goal is to create a large, referenceable bank so you can later say "I like 3, 22, 89, 192" and turn that subset into a sharper product spec.

Current product assumptions from brainstorming:

- Broad audience, but not generic positioning
- Desktop-first web product
- Mobile is not core for typing practice
- Premium, serious, cozy tone
- Subscription later, not one-time purchase
- No microcurrency, no gems, no fake energy systems
- Hybrid structure: guided path plus optional side activities
- Placement step at the start
- Solo-first with light social features later
- Goal is long-term mastery: speed, accuracy, consistency, comfort, and correct habits

## Non-Negotiable Product Principles

1. The game layer should reward real improvement, not empty repetition.
2. The product should feel premium and calm, not loud or manipulative.
3. Practice should create desire to improve, not guilt for missing a day.
4. The world should visualize mastery, not distract from it.
5. Correct technique must matter as much as raw words per minute.
6. Accuracy should be treated as a first-class success metric.
7. The app should make users feel proud, not merely occupied.
8. Progress should be visible in both stats and world state.
9. No microtransactions, loot boxes, spins, gems, or fake scarcity.
10. Quests should feel like meaningful arcs, not daily chores.
